Privacy Policy for Closed Loop CRM

Last Updated: January 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Closed Loop ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your information when you use our CRM mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

To provide the CRM functionality, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address via Firebase Authentication.
  • User-Generated Content: We store the lead data you manually enter, including lead names, phone numbers, email addresses, and financial values.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic technical information (e.g., device model, OS version) to ensure the App runs correctly.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • App Functionality: To display your leads, calculate your ROI, and manage your sales pipeline.
  • Authentication: To keep your account secure and ensure only you can access your data.
  • Service Improvements: To troubleshoot technical issues and improve App performance.
  • Communication: To send you important account updates or security alerts.

Data Storage and Security

We use Google Firebase as our primary service provider for data storage and authentication.

  • Encryption: Your data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard SSL/TLS protocols.
  • Storage: Your lead data is stored in the Google Cloud Firestore database.
  • Protection: We implement Firebase Security Rules to ensure that only authenticated users can access their own data.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information or your lead data to third parties. Data is only shared with Google Firebase for the purpose of hosting the service.

Data Deletion and Retention

You have full control over your data:

  • In-App Deletion: You may delete your account and all associated data at any time via the Settings > Delete Account button. This action is instantaneous and permanent.
  • Manual Deletion: You can delete individual leads within the App at any time.
  • Web Request: If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by emailing closedloopcrm@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
